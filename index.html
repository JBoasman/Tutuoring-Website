Frequently Asked Questions
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for a minimum of 4 hours notice to cancel or reschedule a session. Cancellations made with less than 4 hours notice will be charged the full session fee. We understand that life happens — if something urgent comes up, please get in touch directly and we'll always do our best to accommodate you.
What if I need to reschedule?
Rescheduling is totally fine, provided you give at least 4 hours notice. Simply contact us via email or phone and we'll find a new time that works. Repeat rescheduling with short notice may result in a rescheduling fee.
Where do sessions take place?
Sessions are available both online and in person. Online sessions are conducted via video call (Zoom or Google Meet) — all you need is a device, a stable connection, and somewhere quiet to work. In-person sessions can be arranged depending on your location; please enquire directly to discuss availability.
When are sessions available?
During the school week (Monday to Friday), sessions run from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM NZST — designed to fit around a student's school day. On weekends (Saturday and Sunday), sessions are available from 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM NZST, giving more flexibility for longer or back-to-back bookings.
What year levels and qualifications do you cover?
We tutor across NCEA Levels 1–3, Cambridge IGCSE and A-Level, and IB (SL and HL) for both Physics and Biology. We also offer university bridging support and first-year university assistance, including pre-med preparation for HSFY programmes at Otago and Auckland.
How do I pay?
Payment is collected at the start of each session. For package deals, payment is made upfront at the time of purchase. We accept bank transfer and most major payment methods.
Can two students share a session?
Yes — pair sessions are available for two students booking together. The pair rate is $140 for one hour and $200 for two hours (total, not per student). Both students must be at a compatible level and studying the same qualification for the session to be effective.
Policies
Late arrivals
Sessions start at the booked time. If a student arrives late, the session will still end at the scheduled time. The full session fee applies regardless of arrival time.
What happens if the tutor cancels?
In the rare event we need to cancel a session, we will provide as much notice as possible and offer a full rescheduling at no additional cost. We are committed to reliability and respect your time.
Are sessions recorded?
Sessions are not recorded by default. If you'd like to record a session for personal study review, please let us know in advance so we can discuss the arrangement.
Do you provide notes or resources?
Yes — tutors may provide session summaries, worked examples, or supplementary notes where appropriate. Our Resources page also lists free and purchasable materials to support your study between sessions.
